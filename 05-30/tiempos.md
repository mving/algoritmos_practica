Comparación de tiempos de ordenamiento
======================================

+-----------:+:----------:+:----------:+:----------:+
|            | Mejor      | Peor       | Promedio   |
+------------+------------+------------+------------+
| Selección  | $n^2$      | $n^2$      | $n^2$      |
+------------+------------+------------+------------+
| Inserción  | $n$        | $n^2$      | $n^2$      |
+------------+------------+------------+------------+
| Mergesort  | $n \log n$ | $n \log n$ | $n \log n$ |
+------------+--------------+------------+----------+
| Quicksort  | $n \log n$ | $n^2$      | $n \log n$ |
+------------+------------+------------+------------+
